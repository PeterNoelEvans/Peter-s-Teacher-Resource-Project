Getting Started

  • Students: Register with your assigned username (e.g., Peter41 for class 4/1) and password.
  • Parents: Register with username format "parent-" + child's username (e.g., parent-peter41).
  • After registration, you can:
    • View and manage your portfolio
    • Set your portfolio visibility (public/private)
    • View other students' public portfolios

Need help? Check our registration guide for detailed instructions.
