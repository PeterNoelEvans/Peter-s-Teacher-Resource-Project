Getting Started

  • Students: Register with your name as your username (e.g., Peter)
  • Parents: Register with username format "parent-" + child's username (e.g., parent-peter)
  • Public Visitors: Register with your email address to browse public portfolios

After registration, you can browse portfolios by selecting a school and then a class to explore.

Need help? Check our registration guide for detailed instructions.
