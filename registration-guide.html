Registration Guide

Welcome to the Student Portfolio Hub registration guide. Follow these instructions to create your account based on your user type.

Student Registration

  1. Click the "Register" button on the homepage
  2. Enter your username (your first name, e.g., "Peter42")
  3. Create a secure password
  4. Select your school from the dropdown menu
  5. Select your class
  6. Click "Register" to create your account
Note: Your username should match your name as registered with your school.
Register as Student

Parent Registration

  1. Click the "Register" button on the homepage
  2. Enter your username using the format: "parent-" + your child's username
    • Example: If your child's username is "Peter42", enter "parent-peter42"
  3. Create a secure password
  4. Select your child's school
  5. Select your child's class
  6. Click "Register" to create your account
Note: You'll need to create separate accounts if you have multiple children.
Register as Parent

Public Visitor Registration

  1. Click "Register as Visitor" on the homepage
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Create a secure password
  4. Click "Register" to create your account
Note: Public visitors can only view portfolios that students have made public.
Register as Visitor

After Registration

  • Students:
    • Log in to view and manage your portfolio
    • Set your portfolio visibility (public/private)
    • Update your portfolio content
  • Parents:
    • Log in to view your child's portfolio
    • Monitor your child's progress
  • Public Visitors:
    • Browse schools and classes
    • View public student portfolios

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during registration:

  • Double-check that you've entered your username correctly
  • Make sure you've selected the correct school and class
  • Try clearing your browser cache and cookies
  • Contact your teacher or school administrator for assistance
